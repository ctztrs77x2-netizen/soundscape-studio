Privacy Policy — SoundScape Studio

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Summary

SoundScape Studio is designed with privacy as a core principle. Almost all of your data stays on your device.

What Data We Collect

  • Productivity data (tasks, notepad contents, timer history, sound preferences, themes): Stored 100% locally using Chrome’s storage API. This data never leaves your browser.
  • Premium license key (optional): If you purchase a premium license and voluntarily paste the key into the extension and click “Verify”, the key is sent only to Lemon Squeezy’s license validation API to check if it is valid. We do not store the key ourselves.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect or transmit your browsing history, website content, or any personal communications.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics services.
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone.
  • We do not require an account or login to use the core features.

Third-Party Services

The only external service we communicate with is Lemon Squeezy, and only when you actively choose to verify a premium license key you purchased. Their privacy policy applies to that transaction: https://lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

Your Data, Your Control

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by going to Chrome settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Cookies and other site data (or by uninstalling the extension).

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.

This policy is intentionally short and clear. SoundScape Studio was built to help you focus — not to harvest your data.
